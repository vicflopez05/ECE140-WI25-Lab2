Product Idea

A smart wearable device that continuously monitors heart rate and alerts users to stay at a steady heart rate throughout the day.

Product Description

Our product, Pulse, is a lightweight wristband equipped with sensors that analyze heart rate and activity levels. It syncs with a mobile app to provide personalized activity level and healthy heart rate ranges and highly customizable heart rate tracking to promote better health and prevent health risks for vulnerable individuals.

Customer Interview Validation Results

Interviews with 20 potential users, particularly those with existing health concerns, revealed that 90% are interested in a wearable that tracks heart rate and activity levels. Users emphasized the need for accurate data, real-time alerts for abnormal heart rates, and user-friendly app integration to monitor health effectively.
